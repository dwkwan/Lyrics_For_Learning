About

My name is David Kwan and I am the creator of Lyrics For Learning! I am a former special educator turned software engineer. As a teacher, I found that music was such a powerful way to help my students learn since they all had a natural connection to it.  I wanted to expand upon my experiences and create this educational app to help others practice their English language skills!
